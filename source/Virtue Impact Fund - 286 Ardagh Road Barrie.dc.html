The Thesis

Federal capital builds it. The fund owns the land.

Barrie land, a 40% Build Canada Homes grant and a CMHC MLI Select mortgage combine so a 30%-affordable rental finances on its own income — with the fund contributing land, not cash. The fund's second Barrie site, alongside the already-approved 27 Blake.

Step 01

Contribute land, not cash

The fund puts in the Barrie land. No construction equity, no build risk — the land is the position.

Step 02

Federal capital fills the gap

A 40% Build Canada Homes grant (no repayment) plus a CMHC MLI Select mortgage fund the build at a financeable ~1.6× DSCR.

Step 03

Recover, hold, compound

The fund takes ~$3.5M out at close, keeps a 5% stake, 60% of cash flow and a Year-20 buyout.
