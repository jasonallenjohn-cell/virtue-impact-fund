The Thesis

Federal capital builds it. The fund owns the land.

A 40% Build Canada Homes grant plus a CMHC MLI Select mortgage make affordable housing finance on its own income — with the fund contributing land, not cash. The fund recovers most of its basis at close and keeps the asset, the income and a Year-20 buyout. The same mechanic, repeated across seven Ontario sites.

Capital-light

Grants fund ~95%

Federal grants + senior debt + municipal pieces fund the build — the fund's equity is a small slice.

Capital-back-fast

Basis back at close

The fund recovers ~75% of each land basis at close — capital returns in years, not decades.

Impact + return

Affordable, owned, paid

Permanently affordable homes, an owned asset base, and 60% of cash flow + buyouts.
