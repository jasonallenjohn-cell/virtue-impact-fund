05 · Investment Strategies

Four ways we deploy capital

01
Mezzanine financing

For developers’ raw, entitled, and partially-approved land — provided on a preferred basis with priority repayment, to optimize the capital stack and minimize the weighted-average cost of debt.

02
Joint ventures

Partnering with preeminent real estate developers — under the advice of the Industry Advisor — for tax-efficient capital appreciation on investments.

03
Private & public entities

As a non-primary focus, factoring and investing in equity securities — common shares, preferred shares, and equity investments.

04
Financial instruments

Purchasing and selling instruments to hedge parts of the portfolio against adverse price movements, including foreign-currency exchange risk.
