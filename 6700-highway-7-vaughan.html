Two Paths

Same site. The grant is the unlock.

Path A · Conventional (no BCH)

Built and financed conventionally, on market debt and equity, with no federal grant — the path most comparable mid-rise rental projects follow.

Path B · Virtue / BCH — the unlock

A 40% Build Canada Homes grant plus CMHC MLI Select financing, with the fund contributing the land and holding the asset — delivering 256 non-market homes (~36%) and a Year-20 buyout option.
