Maintain clear space on all sides equal to the height of the emblem’s “V”. Keep this margin free of type, imagery, and other logos.

08 · Graphic Elements

Marks & motifs

Three devices extend the identity. Use them sparingly — one motif per layout.

The Gold Rule

A thin gold line, optionally centred on a dot — for dividers, eyebrows, and section breaks.

The Circle

Wholeness and continuity — a thin ring framing imagery, numerals, or the emblem.

The Emblem

Laurel, open hand, and “V” — reserved for brand moments, never mixed with other icons.

For functional icons (UI, reports), use a single thin-line set at consistent 1.5 px stroke in Slate Navy. No filled or multi-color icons; no emoji.

10 · Disclosure & Legal

Say it carefully

As a mutual fund trust, every public-facing claim carries regulatory weight. Use the approved language below and keep it visible wherever performance, returns, or impact are discussed.

Before publishing

Counsel must review all materials. This page is a brand placeholder, not legal advice.

Standard footnote

This communication is for information only and is not an offer to sell or a solicitation to buy any security. Investments in the Virtue Impact Fund are subject to risk, including possible loss of principal. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

Offering reference

Units are offered only by the Fund’s offering documents, which contain important information about objectives, risks, fees, and expenses. Read them carefully before investing.

Impact claims

Health, wellness, and sustainability outcomes are objectives, not guarantees, and are measured against the Fund’s stated criteria. Figures shown are illustrative unless sourced.
