1Investor capital
Fund-level subscription.

Investor capital is raised into Virtue Impact Fund, then allocated only to the two named opportunities in this deck.

2First deployment
Listowel first.

The first allocation supports Listowel’s modeled $1.03M project equity, alongside senior debt, BCH funding, and grant support.

3Follow-on deployment
