The Operating Layer

Wellness real estate outperforms — when it's actually operated.

Wellness premiums only materialize when a credible operator delivers the experience. Heal House is the live, branded operating layer — a flagship wellness clinic in King City with a real service catalogue and memberships, plus additional Ontario locations.

The fund owns the real estate; Heal is the tenant/operating partner under arm's-length arrangements. Operated wellness turns amenity space from a cost centre into an income and premium driver — and a branded operator de-risks lease-up and stabilizes NOI.
