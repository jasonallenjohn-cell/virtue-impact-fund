Why Integration

Three counterparties, three sets of incentives.

Wellness real estate only works when patient capital, disciplined development, and a wellness operator pull in the same direction. Split across three firms, they rarely do.

The usual way — three firms
—The fund chases yield on a building it did not shape
—The developer builds to spec, takes a fee, and exits
—The operator inherits an asset it never designed
Misaligned incentives, margin leaked between parties, slower stabilization.
The platform way — one roof
—The operator helps design the asset it will run
—The developer builds to operating reality, not just to spec
—The fund underwrites the whole lifecycle, start to stabilized
One thesis, one timeline, aligned economics.
