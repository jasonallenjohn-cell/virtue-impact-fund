Unit Economics

Cheap to acquire. Built to keep.

Insured entry is designed to keep acquisition cost low and member relationships long. The figures below are modeled management assumptions, not realized results.

What keeps acquisition cheap

Members enter through insurance-subsidized consultations, so the platform does not have to buy the first visit. Insured entry removes price friction at the top of the funnel — the model is designed for low acquisition cost relative to the lifetime value of the relationship.

What deepens the relationship

Members who enter through insured care can graduate into cash-pay longevity programs. Membership plus measurable outcomes is intended to drive retention. How members move through this path, and at what value, will only be known as the business matures.

The Venture, Not Just the Clinic

HealConnect — the platform engine.

HealConnect is the connected-care system built on Mazecare — a proven AI-native health-OS connecting patient, provider and insurer: records, real-time eligibility, billing and instant claim adjudication. Providers run it at the level they want: HealConnect Lite layers on top of existing tools; HealConnect Full runs the entire clinic.

Growth Strategy

Three ways to grow locations.

Owned & developed

Track 02 · Lease
Heal Collection

Installed into hotel / landlord space (Sunray + partners). Someone else owns the room and funds the buildout.

Low capital · 90-day pilot · fast · operating margin
Track 03 · Franchise
License the system

License brand, SOPs, platform & training to qualified operators. Fees + royalties + platform revenue, almost no capital per site.

Lowest capital · brand control · fastest · highest margin

The sequence is the strategy: prove it in our real estate + 2 hotel pilots → convert pilots to permanent leases → switch on franchising to scale nationally without scaling capital. Every clinic on HealConnect is one plug-in away — the platform becomes our distribution.
